Here's how I think about design

Empathy, first.

Life mirrors user-centered design in that leading with empathy is better for all

Functionality > Beauty

Design must serve it’s purpose before it can beautiful. But I do love when it’s also pretty...

Always start on paper.

Paper doesn’t distract from the core of design - function and solving user problems.

Design is fundamental

Being a fundamentally sound designer is more important than being a trendy/flashy designer.

Question everything

It’s important to be proud of your work but equally important to forever question it’s effectiveness.

Be a forever learner

Being a student of life and your user doesn’t stop. Never. Stop. Learning.

Champion the user

The user and their goals, constraints, and concerns should be kept front of mind.

Stay lean as long as possible

Solve the essential and maintain awareness of your resources.

Solve problems.

Don’t create them.

Prioritize long-term over short-term success

Build to last, not to skyrocket.

Finish what you start

The world is full of idea people. Your ideas don’t mean anything until they’re shipped.

Less is more

The more you can do, say, show with less, the better. Simplicity is appreciated.
